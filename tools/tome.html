Tome AI

Tome AI is an AI-powered presentation and storytelling platform designed to help users create professional slide decks, visual documents, and pitch presentations quickly. By simply describing the topic, Tome generates structured slides, layouts, and visuals automatically.

Key Features

  • AI powered presentation generator
  • Automatic storytelling slide structure
  • AI generated visuals and layouts
  • Fast pitch deck creation
  • Team collaboration tools

Pricing

Tome AI provides a free plan with basic usage and premium plans for advanced collaboration and features.
