Tome AI

Tome AI is an AI powered storytelling and presentation platform that helps users create presentations, pitch decks, and visual stories instantly using artificial intelligence. It automatically generates layouts, text, and visuals to make professional presentations quickly.

Key Features

  • AI presentation generation
  • Storytelling slide builder
  • AI generated images and layouts
  • Fast document and deck creation
  • Collaboration tools for teams

Pricing

Tome AI offers a free plan with basic features and paid plans for advanced collaboration and AI usage.
