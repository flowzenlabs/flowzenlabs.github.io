HeyGen

HeyGen is an AI video generation platform that enables users to create professional videos with realistic AI avatars and voiceovers using simple text scripts. It is widely used for marketing, education, and business presentations.

Key Features

  • AI avatars for video presentations
  • Text-to-video generation
  • Multiple languages and voiceovers
  • Templates for marketing and training videos
  • Fast AI video production

Pricing

HeyGen offers free trials and paid plans with advanced features, higher video limits, and additional AI avatars.
